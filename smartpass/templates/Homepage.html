ABOUT US

We stop the wait

Long wait lines, they're everywhere, concerts, buses, trains, games etc. People today, living in these fast paced hectic times, struggle to find a way around them and inevitably end up wasting a lot of time. So what our app does is, it stops the wait for you and those around you. Instead of spamming you with mails sending you QR codes, we use your face as the ticket. So, no more wait lines, no more forgetting tickets, struggling to find appropriate mails, just the plain and simple face.
